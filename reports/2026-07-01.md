## Match cards

### England vs DR Congo — England, 1-0 (conf 4)
Market backs England ~75% and Opta agrees (73.9% in 90 mins). Rice returns to a
first-choice midfield, but Reece James (hamstring) and Quansah (ankle) are out and
DR Congo — first knockout in their history — will sit in a 5-3-2 low block around
Wissa's counters. England break it down, but a blowout is unlikely.
- [Opta / The Analyst](https://theanalyst.com/articles/england-vs-dr-congo-prediction-world-cup-2026-match-preview) · [Sports Mole](https://www.sportsmole.co.uk/football/england/world-cup-2026/preview/england-vs-dr-congo-prediction-team-news-lineups_600329.html)

### Belgium vs Senegal — Belgium, 2-1 (conf 3)
The round's most even tie (44/29/27); Opta has Belgium 59% to advance. Lukaku is fit
and scoring (a Belgian WC-record 6 goals) and Trossard created more chances than
anyone in the group stage, against a Senegal defence France and Norway both punished.
Thin lean to Belgium's quality; penalty risk.
- **Disagreement:** sources conflict on keeper Edouard Mendy (knee) — Opta/Sports Mole say he's out (Mory Diaw in), FIFA suggests he passed a fitness test. Either way a downgrade if he sits.
- [Opta / The Analyst](https://theanalyst.com/articles/belgium-vs-senegal-prediction-world-cup-2026-match-preview) · [Sports Mole](https://www.sportsmole.co.uk/football/belgium/world-cup-2026/team-news/belgium-vs-senegal-injury-suspension-list-predicted-xis_600369.html)

### USA vs Bosnia & Herzegovina — USA, 2-0 (conf 4)
Co-hosts firm favourites (~69%); Pochettino calls it a final as the USA chase a first
knockout win in 24 years. Pulisic returns from a calf knock and Balogun is restored,
while Bosnia — first-ever WC knockout — carry a thin ~1.87 xG-per-game attack led by
40-year-old Dzeko. Crowd, depth and pressing tell.
- [Sports Mole](https://www.sportsmole.co.uk/football/usa/world-cup-2026/team-news/usa-vs-bosnia-herzegovina-injury-suspension-list-predicted-xis_600325.html) · [Covers](https://www.covers.com/world-cup/usa-vs-bosnia-prediction-picks-odds-wednesday-7-1-2026)

### Spain vs Austria — Spain, 2-0 (conf 4)
Tournament favourites (~72%) yet to concede a goal. Nico Williams (adductor) and Pino
are doubts and Yamal carries a knock, thinning the wide options, but the depth dwarfs
an Austria side with no clean sheet in 12 WC matches who shipped three to Algeria.
Consensus is a controlled win-to-nil, not a rout.

### Portugal vs Croatia — Portugal, 2-1 (conf 3)
A Ronaldo-vs-Modric duel with Portugal modest favourites (~54%) and a fully fit squad.
Both attacks misfired in the group stage and Croatia are masters at dragging knockouts
level — five of their last seven WC knockouts were level after 90. Portugal's depth
edges a low-scoring one; high penalty risk.
- **Disagreement:** Racing Post's best bet is the draw (13/5), citing Portugal's two goalless knockout draws under Martinez; previews lean Under 2.5.
