## Spain vs Argentina — the FINAL (Sun 19 Jul, 19:00 UTC)

**Pick: Spain (home) · 2-1 · confidence 3/5**

The market frames the final as a narrow Spain win: **41% Spain, 32% draw, 27% Argentina**. Spain arrive on a six-match winning streak having conceded just **one goal in the entire tournament**, with Yamal running the tournament's most incisive attack. But Argentina are the value side and anything but a soft opponent — the highest scorers here (17 goals), the defending champions, and **Messi now leads the Golden Boot race** (level with Mbappé on eight goals, ahead on assists) after teeing up both goals in the late comeback against England. Spain's suffocating defence versus Argentina's firepower and Scaloni's game-changing bench (Lautaro, Julián Álvarez) points to a tight, open final that Spain just shade — consistent with our locked **champion pick of Spain**.

**Disagreement / risk:** Tipsters flag Argentina as the genuine value bet — holders with tournament-winning DNA, a vintage Messi and a habit of stoppage-time comebacks (Enzo + Lautaro vs England). Both-teams-to-score is widely expected and over 90 minutes this is close to a coin-flip that could swing Argentina's way or into extra time.
