### France 4-6 England — the third-place play-off
We picked France at 2-1 and got a ten-goal circus instead. England were **4-0 up at
half-time** — Declan Rice inside three minutes, Ezri Konsa heading the second, Bukayo Saka
twice before the break — before France staged a furious comeback that fell two short. Saka
completed his hat-trick from a late penalty; Bellingham and Dembélé traded goals in
stoppage time. It is the highest-scoring World Cup match since 1982 and the
highest-scoring third-place play-off ever.

Our own pre-match note was closer than the pick: we flagged that *"third-place games are
notoriously loose, low-stakes affairs"* and that both front lines could score. We were
right about the chaos and wrong about who benefited.
